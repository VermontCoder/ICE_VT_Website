Welcome to ICE Tours VT! We're a Vermont-based group that organizes tourist-style bus tours of ICE facilities in the Burlington area. Think Hollywood star tours, but instead of celebrity mansions you get federal surveillance infrastructure. It's a little strange, a little funny, and entirely real.

Our mission is to shine a light on the immigration enforcement apparatus quietly operating in our communities. By showing up, looking around, and asking questions, we help Vermonters understand what's happening in their own backyard.
