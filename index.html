Welcome to ICE Tours VT

Discover what's happening right here in Vermont! ICE Tours VT offers guided tours of Immigration and Customs Enforcement facilities in the Burlington area. Learn about the infrastructure, operations, and technology behind federal immigration enforcement in our own backyard.

Our tours provide an eye-opening look at the surveillance and enforcement apparatus that operates in our community every day.
